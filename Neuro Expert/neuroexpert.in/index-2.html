Dr. Vikas Gupta

Dr. Vikas Gupta Director and Head of Neurosurgery and Neuro interventional surgery at Max Super Specialty Hospital. Dr. Vikas Gupta is known as a reliable specialist for complex neurosurgeries in the country. He is among the few neurosurgeons in India who are formally trained in micro-neurosurgery as well as neurointerventional surgery. His passion is neurovascular and acute stroke.

  • • Mainly focused at minimal invasive surgeries through endoscopes in brain and spine.
  • • He was trained in G.B.P.G.I.M.E.R and MAMC, New Delhi in intervention neurology from various centers in India, Zurich and USA.
  • • He is also credited for separating six months old conjoined twins through microsurgery for the first time in Indian Medical history.
