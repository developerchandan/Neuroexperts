What is Brain Injury

Vikas Gupta 2021-03-20

The term brain injury used when there is an impact on the head that interferes with the normal functioning of the brain. Brain injury has unpredictable consequences and results.

Everything can be changed in a matter of seconds.

Brain Injuries can define into two types.

Traumatic and Non-Traumatic

Traumatic brain injury can be caused by car or motorcycle crashes, falls, sports injuries, and assaults. It is sudden damage to the brain caused by a blow or jolt to the head.
Non-Traumatic brain injury can be caused by cardiac arrest, near-drowning experience, oxygen deprivation, metabolic disorders, aneurysms, and illness. They are injuries to the brain that are not caused by an external physical force to the head.
